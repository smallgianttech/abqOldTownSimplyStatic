Ronn Perea signs his historical novel “Elsie and Elsa” and his fictional memoir “How I Got My Kicks on Route 66” on Sunday, December 9 from 1:30 to 3:30 pm at treasure House Books & Gifts, 2012 South Plaza NW in Old Town, Albuquerque, NM. The event is free and open to the public. For more information, call (505) 242-7204.
\nELSIE AND ELSA
\nDuring the mid-20th Century, many historical events occurred in the American Southwest that shaped the lives of many families. This story brings to light those events and the people that changed the course of history.
\nWe will visit WWII secret prisoner of war camps, the atomic bomb espionage, the embryonic birth of technology, the Pope, the President, movie stars, celebrities, and a future rock-n-roll sex god.
\nAs this story takes place a life shaping relationship will grow between two high school girls Elsie and Elsa\u2026
\nHow I Got My Kicks on Route 66
\n(a.k.a. Smiles Giggles and Laughs)
\nRonn Greco is a producer, a song and dance man at his dinner theatre\u2015well-known for food, drinks, and live entertainment of romantic comedy. Yet, he has an overwhelming urge to produce something fresher!
\nHe discovers local crowd-pleasing shows that are filled with individuals, duos, males, and females of all ethnicities to perform in his new club, The Duke City Comedy Club.
\nYears pass when tragedy strikes\u2014he thinks his comedy club years have passed and wonders if he will ever laugh again\u2026<\/p>\n\n\t

Join us for Our Twenty Years \u201cThank You Celebration\u201d!<\/p>\n

The Albuquerque Photographers’ Gallery has been part of our community for twenty years! An artist-owned business, we’ve been in Old Town serving fine art collectors from Albuquerque, greater New Mexico, and the world.<\/p>\n

The party will be from 5-9pm<\/strong> on the Friday 12 and all day<\/strong> on the 13th and 14th.<\/p>\n

The gallery is located at 528 San Felipe Suite B in Old Town<\/p>\n\n\t

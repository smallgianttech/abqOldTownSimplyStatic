Come join us at the most exciting car show in Albuquerque! Get ready to be blown away by the stunning display of classic and custom cars from all eras. Experience the thrill of seeing beautifully restored vintage cars alongside some of the most incredible classics. Our car show is a celebration of automotive culture, showcasing the passion and craftsmanship that goes into these amazing machines. With food, drinks, and beautiful cars there’s something for everyone to enjoy. Don’t miss out on this incredible event in Old Town Albuquerque at the Albuquerque Museum. Join us for a day of fun and excitement that you’ll never forget!<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Description<\/strong>
Albuquerque Museum and the New Mexico Council of Car Clubs host the 37th Annual NMCCC Classic Auto Show. This year\u2019s theme will celebrate tail fins. Antique, classic, exotic and sports cars, along with trucks, motorcycles and vintage camper trailers will be on display in the Museum parking lot. Automobile art and model vehicles will be exhibited in the Special Events room inside the Museum.

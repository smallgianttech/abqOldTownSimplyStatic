Lapis Room and Soulstice Events welcome you to SIP Amarillo. Join us for live world-music by Eli Del Puerto, and a demonstration on sacred heart symbolism and metalsmithing techniques by Selina Baca of Desert Goddess. Small bites by Honey Bee Kitchen and house-made signature cocktails\/mocktails to be provided.<\/p>\n

Limited tickets available, with early bird specials until Sunday Oct 22nd, secure yours at www.lapisroom.com. <\/p>\n

SIP AMARILLO will be Friday, October 27th, from 7:00 pm – 9:00 pm at Lapis Room art gallery (303 Romero St NW S107, Albuquerque, NM 87104).<\/p>\n

SIP is a series of events hosted at Lapis Room. Future dates + themes include:<\/p>\n

SIP NARANJA – Friday, November 17th, 7-9pm
\nSIP FUEGO – Friday, December 15th, 7-9pm
\nSIP CIELO – Friday, January 19th, 7-9pm<\/p>\n\n\t

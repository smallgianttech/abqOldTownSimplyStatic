Larada Horner-Miller signs “Hair on Fire: A Heart-Warming & Humorous Christmas Memoir” on Saturday, December 2 from 1:30 to 3:30 pm at Treasure House Books & Gifts, 2012 South Plaza NW in Old Town, Albuquerque, NM. The event is free and open to the public. For more information call (505) 242-7204.
\nTired of focusing on the stressful hustle and bustle of December? These delightful stories brim with Christmas nostalgia and heartwarming moments.
\nAre you hoping to rediscover the magic of the winter holidays? Looking for traditional inspiration for your upcoming Xmas parties? Ever wondered what\u2019s behind the twelve days of gift-giving? The daughter of a real cowboy, award-winning author Larada Horner-Miller grew up in a small rural community in southeastern Colorado. Now she\u2019s using her seventy years of festive experiences to share the true meaning of the season and how to rejoice in its miracles.
\nHair on Fire is a compilation of poems, prose, and helpful scripture references all centered around family. Using vivid and humorous language, Horner-Miller reveals a treasure trove of tales that will touch anyone\u2019s heart. And when you immerse yourself in the warmth of her stories, you\u2019ll soon be looking forward to the big day with refreshed anticipation and pure happiness.
\nIn Hair on Fire, you\u2019ll enjoy:
\n– A vibrant celebration of Christmas specialness that will reawaken your inner child
\n– At look at the mix of sadness and joy that the 25th of December can trigger and why it\u2019s okay to embrace them both
\n– An immersive retelling of what Joseph and Mary experienced on the road to Bethlehem, so you can put yourself in the Holy Family\u2019s shoes.
\n– What the traditions of a New Mexican holiday entail and how to bring them to life
\n– The reason you should never stand too close to the angels in a pageant, and much, much more!
\nHair on Fire is a charming collection of Christmas memories told through poetry and prose. If you like uplifting journeys, evocative memoirs, and light-hearted joy, then you\u2019ll love Larada Horner-Miller\u2019s Yuletide gift.
\nLarada Horner-Miller is a poet, essayist and accomplished multi-genre author who holds a bachelor\u2019s degree in English, with a minor in Spanish and a master of education degree in Integrating Technology into the Classroom. She is the accomplished author of six award-winning biographies, historical fiction, memoir, and poetry works plus three self-published cookbooks.<\/p>\n\n\t

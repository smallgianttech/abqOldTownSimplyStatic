Dale A. Garratt signs his novel “The Peace Road: A High-Stakes Geopolitical Thriller” on Sunday December 10 from 1:30 to 3:30 pm at Treasure House Books & Gifts, 2012 South Plaza NW in Old Town, Albuquerque, NM. The event is free and open to the public. For more information, call (505) 242-7204.
\nNorth Korea launches a hypersonic missile that barely misses Los Angeles. U.S. President Sarah Jacobsen tasks top physicist Ric O\u2019Malley to quickly complete a quantum computer (QC) project. Running 150 million times faster than any existing computer, this QC will bring Artificial Intelligence (AI) to a level that can stop any ICBM in the world. Ric races to East Asia to obtain breakthrough research data from South Korean and Japanese allies.
\nDale A. Garratt taught English at the secondary and university levels in California, Connecticut and his home state of New Mexico. While teaching in South Korea for eight years, he travelled in the People\u2019s Republic of China and Japan as well as throughout South Korea. The closest he has gotten to North Korea is standing on the North Korean side of the UN Quonset hut that straddles the 38th parallel in the Joint Security Area of Panmunjom. Dale and his wife Joy reside in Albuquerque<\/p>\n\n\t

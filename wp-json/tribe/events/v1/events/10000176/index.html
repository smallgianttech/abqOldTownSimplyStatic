Jesse Littlebird \/ Lapis Room Workshop + Group Show<\/p>\n

Let Littlebird Guide You from Blank Canvas to Exhibition!<\/p>\n

Over two vision workshops, July 6th and July 13th from 6:00 pm – 9:00 pm,  Jesse Littlebird will guide you through the process of finding the starting place, acknowledging hang ups, and deploying breakthrough techniques, while transcending through themes of ego, impermanence and perfectionism. Littlebird will lead visitors through daily, durational performances and exercises. During these sessions, he will lead participants through a series of “Coyote Strategies” to immerse them in his own personal art practice.<\/p>\n

During the month of July, Participants will be challenged to complete a 24 x 36″ canvas to display at an August group show at Fusion Theatre, alongside Jesse Littlebird\u2019s large scale works.<\/p>\n

Participation is $125: includes 2 guided workshops, a month-long group show opportunity and a 24 x 36″ canvas.

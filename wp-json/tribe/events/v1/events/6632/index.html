Santacruzan in Old Town!<\/p>\n\n\n\n

The Santacruzan is a ritual pageant held in May and this year it\u2019s on Saturday, May 20 from 4-8 p.m. in Historic Old Town. The Filipino American Foundation of New Mexico presents this free cultural experience for the public to enjoy.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Join them in a celebration of Flores de Mayo!<\/p>\n\n\n
